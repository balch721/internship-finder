About this project

Aryan's Internship Finder is a personal project I built to make finding the right summer internship feel less like guesswork. Instead of scrolling endless job boards, you can take a quick survey to get a ranked, personalized shortlist — or explore the whole database by field.

Every listing is researched with real company details: tiers, competitiveness, estimated pay, company values, stock and recent news, plus the majors each role actually recruits — so your matches reflect where you'd genuinely fit.

It's a work in progress, with more fields going live over time. Thanks for stopping by.
